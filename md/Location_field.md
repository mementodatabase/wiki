---
title: "Location field"
---

A Location field may contain a location, depicted as a latitude/longitude pair of integers.

A Location may be established as the current location or one obtained by pointing to a place on a map.

A Location may have an associated text field containing a postal address.

A Location field value is exported & imported as two integers (a latitude & a longitude) separated by a comma & space (", ").

   1. Advanced Parameters
**Rapid determination of coordinates** — Choose between rapid action and higher accuracy.

**Separate field for the address** — By default, there is none; otherwise, either a name or a purpose may be specified.
