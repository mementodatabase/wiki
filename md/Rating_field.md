---
title: "Rating field"
---

A Rating field may be empty or contain an integer value from 0 to 10.

A Rating field is exported and imported as an integer.

   1. Advanced Parameters
**Maximum number of stars** — By default, a rating has a maximum of 5 stars. An alternate value from 2 to 10 may be entered.

**Default Value** — By default, the default value for a eating is 0. An alternate value from 0 to 10 may be selected.

**Tooltips** — For each of the possible values of a rating, a string may be entered to act as a tooltip for that rating.
