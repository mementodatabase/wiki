---
title: "Text field"
---

A Text field may contain any textual data. It is stored in UTF-8, a standard 8-bit Unicode format.

   1. Advanced Parameters
**Default Value** — By default, there is no default value. Otherwise, one may be specified.

**Single-line text?** — Yes or no

**Autocomplete** — Yes or no
If Yes, then when creating or editing an entry, values entered in previously-created entries will be presented for this field after matching the first two letters for selection as the value for the field.

**Uppercase** — There are 4 choices for the handling of case in this Text field:
- No (None)
- In the beginning of the sentence
- In the beginning of each word
- All letters: All capital letters
