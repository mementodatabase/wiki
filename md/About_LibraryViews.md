title: "About:LibraryViews"
---

There are several views of library entries available in Memento. Which are available depends on the edition of the app you are running. In the Mobile edition, the available views are [List view](About:Glossary#L.md), [Cards view](About:Glossary#C.md), [Table view](About:Glossary#T.md), [Calendar view](About:Glossary#C.md), and [Kanban view](About:Glossary#K.md).
